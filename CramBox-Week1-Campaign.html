04 — DISTRIBUTIONChannel Priority Stack

Tier 0 — Owned by the Product (Free, Automatic)

Score cards on user WhatsApp Status — every active user with a decent score now distributes for you. Zero cost, zero effort. The whole campaign should reinforce "share what you score."

The countdown — every day the number drops by 1. That's a free re-engagement message inside the product. Use it in your weekly recap posts ("the app says 143 today").

Tier 1 — Highest Leverage (Do These First)

Personal WhatsApp broadcast to everyone in your contacts with a Form 4-age child. Direct, trusted, no algorithm. Every person you message directly is worth 10x a Facebook post.

Active school WhatsApp groups — get into 3-5 Form 4 class groups and post the student-facing copy. One message to a 50-student group is your most efficient distribution unit.

Teachers — one teacher with 40 students is worth 40 individual messages. The free-code offer (30 codes for a group broadcast) is the trade.

Tier 2 — Volume (Do These Consistently)

Facebook personal + groups — Zimbabwean Parents groups, Harare community groups, school-specific Facebook groups. Parent copy in parent spaces, student copy in student spaces.

WhatsApp Status — post daily yourself. Repost notable student score cards (with permission).

TikTok — the demo video and the score-card compilation. No face needed.

Tier 3 — Later (Week 2+)

Facebook paid ads — run the parent-facing copy targeting Zimbabwe, age 30-55. $10-20 budget. Only after organic message is converting.

Instagram — lower priority; Facebook + WhatsApp are where Zimbabwean parents live.

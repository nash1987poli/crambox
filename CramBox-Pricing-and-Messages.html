Why $5 (not $3) now: the product is worth more than at launch — an AI exam coach (the Invigilator), Team Spar group study, the full syllabus, all offline. At $5 you're still under 1/6 of a single $30 tutor lesson. Always show the value before the price.

The golden rules

  • Never show "$5" naked. Lead with the value (AI coach + whole syllabus + offline), then the price lands cheap.
  • Anchor against the alternative: "$30+ for one tutor hour" or "exam stakes" make $5 vanish.
  • Push the Squad for groups. It's cheaper per head and it spreads the app — exactly how Zimbabwean students study.
  • Let scores do the selling. The in-app "Challenge a friend" card carries the price + link automatically.

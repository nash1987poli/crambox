How this is calculated

Visits/week: 500 (Week 1 organic reach from the campaign).
Conversion curve: exponential decay — $1 ≈ 65%, $3 ≈ 30%, $6 ≈ 12%, $10 ≈ 5%. Models impulse-buy friction.
Share rate: proportion of activated users who post a score card to WhatsApp Status. Higher at lower prices (pride + low regret).
Reach per share: 150 contacts (avg WA personal list in target demo).
Viral coefficient: how many new activations each existing user produces. 1.0+ = self-sustaining growth.
These are directional models, not forecasts. Tune them once you have real data.

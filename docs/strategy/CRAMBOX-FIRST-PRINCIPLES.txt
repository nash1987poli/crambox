CRAMBOX_READINESS_FIRST_PRINCIPLES.md Purpose
This document defines the primary strategic objective of Crambox. Every feature, screen, workflow, AI system, curriculum decision, and business decision must support this objective. Failure to follow these principles risks turning Crambox into a generic EdTech platform.
The Core Mission
Crambox exists to answer one question: "How ready am I for the exam, and what should I do next?"
Everything else is secondary.
What Crambox Is Not
Crambox is NOT: · A notes repository · A video platform · A tutoring marketplace · An AI chatbot · A question bank · A content library
Those may exist inside Crambox. They are not the product.
What Crambox Is
Crambox is:

Readiness Definition
Readiness is a weighted estimate of a student's ability to achieve their target grade in the examination. Readiness should be calculated using:
· Topic mastery · Difficulty weighting · ZIMSEC frequency · Historical performance · Assessment performance · Consistency
ZIMSEC Intelligence Principle
Past papers are not content. Past papers are data. The purpose of past paper analysis is:
· Frequency analysis · Marks weighting · Difficulty modelling · Readiness weighting
6

The purpose is NOT: · Copying questions · Reproducing papers
Voice Tutor Principle
Voice explanations are not the product. Voice explanations exist to improve readiness. Correct:
Improve Readiness  Listen To Explanation
Incorrect:
Voice Tutor  Everything Else
AI Principle
AI must never become the core product. AI exists to support:
· Readiness · Personalization · Explanations · Recommendations The readiness engine remains the source of truth.
Founder Decision Rule
When faced with two competing priorities: Choose the option that increases readiness accuracy.

The Crambox Promise
Students should never wonder: "Am I ready?"
Crambox should always answer: "This is your current readiness. This is your target. This is what is stopping you. This is what to do next."
That is the product. Everything else is support infrastructure.
